# Overview

Preparation comes down to three things: layering for cold pre-dawn starts
(viewpoints can sit around 5–15°C), good grip for volcanic sand and canyon
steps, and waterproofing where you will get wet. Fitness needs vary by
destination — Mount Bromo suits most fitness levels, while the Ijen crater
hike and canyon descents are more demanding. JVTO provides gas masks and
trekking poles on Ijen.
