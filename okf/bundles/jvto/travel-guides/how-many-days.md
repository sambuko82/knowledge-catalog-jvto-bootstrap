# Overview

The right trip length depends on how many destinations you want and where
you start. Short itineraries focus on a single Bromo sunrise; adding days
brings in Kawah Ijen, the Tumpak Sewu and Madakaripura waterfalls, and the
Papuma coast on the longest circuits. See the itinerary guide for what each
duration covers, day by day.
