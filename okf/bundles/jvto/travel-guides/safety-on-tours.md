# Overview

JVTO treats safety as a written framework applied before and during every
tour rather than a marketing line: pre-hike safety briefings, trained
guides, proper equipment, and written contingency ("Plan-B") procedures. The
approach reflects the founder's background as a serving Tourist Police
officer.
