# Overview

The two signature volcanoes suit different travelers. **Mount Bromo**
(about 2,329 m) is a sunrise-and-caldera experience with little sustained
hiking — a 4WD jeep does most of the work. **Kawah Ijen** (about 2,386 m) is
a strenuous pre-dawn hike rewarded by the "blue fire" and a turquoise acidic
crater lake. Many travelers do both on a multi-day route.
