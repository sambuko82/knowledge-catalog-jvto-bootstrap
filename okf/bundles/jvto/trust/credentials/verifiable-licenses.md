# Credential

JVTO publishes its credentials so they can be independently verified, including:

- business registration (NIB 1102230032918) and tourism registration (TDUP);
- conservation-authority (BBKSDA) clearance to operate at Bromo and Ijen;
- official ISIC provider status (provider ID 259268);
- the founder's serving Tourist Police role.

The public verification page lists these with their checkable sources.
