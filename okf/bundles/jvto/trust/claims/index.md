# Trust/Claims

## Concepts

* [Ijen Medical Screening Included](medical-screening.md) - A health certificate is mandatory for every guest before Kawah Ijen crater entry; JVTO coordinates the screening with a licensed doctor, included in the tour.
* [No Hidden Costs](no-hidden-costs.md) - JVTO tours are all-inclusive of transport, crew, entrance fees, water, and stated meals, with no on-the-day surprise charges.
* [Police-Led Operations](police-led.md) - JVTO is founded and led by a serving member of the Indonesian Tourist Police, and publishes verifiable documentation of that role.
* [100% Private Tours](private-format.md) - Every JVTO tour is fully private — a dedicated vehicle, driver, and guide, with no shared groups.
