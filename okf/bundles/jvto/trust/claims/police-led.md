# Claim

JVTO's founder, Agung Sambuko, is a serving officer of the Indonesian
Tourist Police, and the company runs a safety-led, "police-led" approach to
volcano travel.

# How It Is Shown Publicly

- JVTO publishes its police-assignment documentation and an independent press
  record on its public verification page.
- The operating company, PT Java Volcano Rendezvous, is registered under
  Indonesian business number (NIB) 1102230032918, verifiable on the national
  OSS portal.
