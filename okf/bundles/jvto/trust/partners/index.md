# Trust/Partners

## Concepts

* [Himpunan Pelaku Wisata Khusus Ijen (HPWKI)](hpwki.md) - JVTO publicly presents HPWKI as a local volcanic-tourism industry and competence context around Ijen.
* [INDECON (Indonesia Ecotourism Network)](indecon.md) - JVTO is featured in INDECON's public Spotlight Networks listing of the Indonesia Ecotourism Network.
* [ISIC (International Student Identity Card)](isic.md) - JVTO is listed as a provider in ISIC's public discount directory (provider ID 259268), the basis for its student pricing on selected packages.
