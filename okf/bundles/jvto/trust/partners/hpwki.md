# Relationship

JVTO publicly presents HPWKI as a partner and as local industry context for
volcanic-tourism competence around Ijen. A public JVTO-hosted approval artifact
is retained as supporting first-party documentation.

BBKSDA Jawa Timur separately documented training conducted with HPWKI for Ijen
guides in May 2024. The training covered conservation, guide responsibilities,
Search and Rescue, and emergency first aid.

# Claim Boundary

This concept records JVTO's publicly presented HPWKI relationship and the broader
professional-training context of HPWKI.
It does not establish that BBKSDA directly certified JVTO, that every JVTO guide
attended the training, or that all JVTO operations are approved by BBKSDA.
