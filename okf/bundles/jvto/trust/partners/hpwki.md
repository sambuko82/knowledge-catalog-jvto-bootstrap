# Overview

HPWKI (Himpunan Pelaku Wisata Khusus Ijen) is an association of
special-interest tourism actors and guides operating in the Kawah Ijen area.
Its role as an Ijen guide association is independently corroborated by the
East Java Conservation Authority (BBKSDA Jawa Timur), which ran guide training
for HPWKI members in May 2024.

# Relationship to JVTO

JVTO states on its own website that it holds membership in HPWKI and lists the
association registration AHU-0001072.AH.01.07.TAHUN 2024. The membership and
that registration number are self-reported by JVTO and have not been
independently confirmed via the AHU registry.
