# Relationship

JVTO publicly presents HPWKI (Himpunan Pelaku Wisata Khusus Ijen) as a partner
and as local industry context for volcanic-tourism competence around Ijen. A
public JVTO-hosted approval artifact is retained as supporting first-party
documentation.

BBKSDA Jawa Timur separately documented training conducted with HPWKI for Ijen
guides in May 2024, covering conservation, guide responsibilities, Search and
Rescue, and emergency first aid.

# Claim Boundary

This concept records JVTO's publicly presented HPWKI relationship and the
broader professional-training context of HPWKI. The membership and the cited
association registration number are self-reported by JVTO and not independently
confirmed via the AHU registry. It does not establish that BBKSDA directly
certified JVTO, that every JVTO guide attended the training, or that all JVTO
operations are approved by BBKSDA.
