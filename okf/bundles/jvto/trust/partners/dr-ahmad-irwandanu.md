# Relationship

On its own public pages, JVTO identifies Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu as the doctor
associated with its Ijen pre-hike health-screening coordination, where such

# Claim Boundary

This concept records an operating relationship for applicable Ijen screening
coordination, as stated on JVTO's own pages. It does not confirm the doctor's
current license validity, expiry date, private-practice details, or a universal
screening requirement. A public Credential concept will be created only if and
when a directly public official registry record supports the exact
professional-status claim.
