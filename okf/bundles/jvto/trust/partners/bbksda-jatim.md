# Overview

Balai Besar KSDA Jawa Timur (BBKSDA Jawa Timur) is the East Java conservation
authority that manages the Kawah Ijen nature-tourism area, including its
official online "Ijen Blue Fire" e-ticketing and cashless (QRIS) system and
guide-training programmes.

# Relationship to JVTO

As with any operator working in the area, JVTO's Ijen tours are subject to
BBKSDA Jawa Timur's access, ticketing, and visitor rules. BBKSDA is the
governing conservation authority for Ijen, not a commercial partner of JVTO.
JVTO states on its own website that it holds "BBKSDA operator clearance" for
Bromo and Ijen; that specific clearance is a first-party claim and is not
independently listed on BBKSDA's public site.
