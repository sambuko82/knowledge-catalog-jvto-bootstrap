# Overview

Java Volcano Tour Operator is listed on the ISIC / Totum discount directory as
a participating discount provider (provider id 259268), where ISIC
student/youth/teacher cardholders can redeem an operator-set discount applied
at JVTO checkout.

# Relationship to JVTO

JVTO describes itself as collaborating with ISIC to offer student-friendly
pricing on selected tours. Availability of specific student packages can vary
(the JVTO student page may show no packages at a given time). No discount
percentage is asserted here; confirm current terms on the ISIC/Totum listing
and at booking.
