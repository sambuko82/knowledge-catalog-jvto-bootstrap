# Relationship

JVTO is listed as a provider in ISIC's public discount directory under
provider ID 259268. JVTO uses ISIC verification (Alive Verify API) to
confirm student status for its student pricing on selected packages.

# Claim Boundary

This concept records a public ISIC provider-directory listing only. It
does not establish a certification, endorsement, corporate membership, or
partnership beyond that listing.
