# Overview

Weather-dependent sights — a clear sunrise, or Ijen's blue fire — cannot be
guaranteed, because cloud, weather, and volcanic gas are outside any
operator's control. JVTO plans timing and routes to maximise the chance of
good conditions, and may adjust timing or routing for safety.
