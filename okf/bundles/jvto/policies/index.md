# Policies

## Concepts

* [Booking Safety & Anti-Fraud](anti-fraud.md) - Use only JVTO's official channels; JVTO never asks for full card numbers, CVV, banking passwords, or OTP codes.
* [How to Book](booking-paths.md) - JVTO's two official booking paths — website Instant Book and WhatsApp-assisted booking.
* [Cancellation & Lifetime Travel Credit](cancellation-travel-credit.md) - How JVTO handles guest-initiated cancellations — Travel Credit instead of cash refunds, on a 48-hour cut-off.
* [Ijen Health Screening](ijen-health-screening.md) - For Ijen crater hikes, JVTO coordinates a health screening with licensed medical staff when BBKSDA regulations require it.
* [What's Included](inclusions-exclusions.md) - JVTO tours are all-inclusive of transport, crew, entrance fees, water, and stated meals; flights, insurance, tips, and personal expenses are excluded.
* [ISIC Student Pricing](isic-student.md) - JVTO states it offers ISIC student pricing on selected packages (provider id 259268); the public ISIC listing was not independently confirmed to display JVTO.
* [Natural Phenomena Are Not Guaranteed](natural-phenomena.md) - JVTO cannot guarantee weather-dependent sights such as sunrise or Ijen's blue fire; itineraries are planned to maximise the chance.
* [Payment & Deposit](payment-rules.md) - A deposit secures the booking, with the balance due before the tour; exact amounts and deadlines are on the booking page.
* [Police Escort for Large Groups](police-escort.md) - For large groups, JVTO can coordinate an official traffic-police escort on some road segments, subject to authority approval.
