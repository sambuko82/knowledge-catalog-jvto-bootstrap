# Overview

JVTO accepts bookings only through the official JVTO website. Choose a package,
date, and number of travelers, review the price, accept the terms, and pay — the
booking is recorded under a valid JVTO Booking ID.

WhatsApp and email are for customer assistance only (questions about custom
routes, groups, or complex pick-up/drop-off). They do not create, confirm,
modify, cancel, or transfer a booking; every booking action is completed on the
website.
