# Overview

JVTO offers two official ways to book:

- **Website Instant Book** — choose a package, date, and number of travelers,
  review the price, accept the terms, and pay.
- **WhatsApp-assisted booking** — for custom routes or special requests; JVTO
  confirms availability and price and sends a payment link.

Book only through JVTO's official channels.
