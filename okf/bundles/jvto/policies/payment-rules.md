# Overview

A deposit (standard 20% of the booking value) secures a booking, with the
balance due before the tour starts; close-to-departure bookings may require
fuller payment. Exact amounts, accepted methods, and deadlines are shown at
checkout and on the booking information page.

Pay only through JVTO's official payment links and listed accounts.
