# Overview

Tourism Nature Park (TWA) Kawah Ijen closes completely to all tourism and
mining activity on the **first Friday of every month** (00:00–23:59 WIB) for
the **Rijik** ecosystem-cleaning program, which has run since March 2019.

# What Happens

On the closure day the conservation authority (BBKSDA/BKSDA), SAR, and HPWKI
jointly remove an estimated 100–150 kg of waste from Paltuding up to the crater
rim and plant endemic Cemara Gunung (*Casuarina junghuhniana*) to stabilise the
trail slopes and limit erosion. The crater is not accessible to visitors that day.

# Booking Impact

JVTO schedules tours around the monthly closure, so an Ijen hike is never booked
on a Rijik day — guests booked through JVTO are not affected.
