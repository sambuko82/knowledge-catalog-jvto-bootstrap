# Reference

A 2021 Radar Jember report describes a Tourist Police and Disparpora environmental
patrol related to agricultural waste, odor, and area cleanliness in the Bondowoso
district.

# Claim Boundary

This is a historical press record of an environmental-cleanliness patrol.
It is not evidence of sulfur monitoring, gas monitoring, medical screening, or
crater-risk control.
