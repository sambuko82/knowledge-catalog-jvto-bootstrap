# Overview

https://javavolcano-touroperator.com is the official first-party website of
Java Volcano Tour Operator (JVTO), which presents itself as a private,
Tourist Police-led volcano tour operator based in East Java, Indonesia. It is
the operator's primary public domain, where JVTO publishes its tour packages,
policies, and contact details directly.

# Note on Evidence

This is a first-party source: it documents what JVTO states about itself. No
government or registrar record was located that independently ties this domain
to the registered entity, so any claim sourced only here is "as stated by the
operator" rather than independently verified.
