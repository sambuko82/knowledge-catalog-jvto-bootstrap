# Reference

BBKSDA Jawa Timur documented training for guides affiliated with HPWKI from
20 to 22 May 2024 at TWA Kawah Ijen. The report describes training on
conservation, local-guide responsibilities, Search and Rescue, and
Penanggulangan Penderita Gawat Darurat (emergency first aid).

# Claim Boundary

This source supports HPWKI training context.
It does not establish that JVTO was directly certified by BBKSDA, that a specific
JVTO employee attended, or that every JVTO guide completed the training.
