# Person

Agung Sambuko ("Mr. Sam") founded JVTO and is a serving officer of the Indonesian
Tourist Police. JVTO's "police-led" safety model and crew-accountability chain are
built around his role; independent press named "Bripka Agung Sambuko" in
tourist-police duties in 2021.
