# Person

JVTO identifies Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu as the doctor associated with its applicable
Ijen pre-hike health-screening coordination. JVTO states that Ijen night-hike
packages include a pre-hike health screening as part of the route logistics.

# Claim Boundary

This records an operating relationship for applicable Ijen screening coordination
only. It does not confirm current medical-license validity, expiry date,
private-practice details, or a universal screening requirement. Whether screening
is required or verified before crater access depends on current local authority or
provider rules.
