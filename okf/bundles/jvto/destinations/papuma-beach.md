# Overview

Papuma Beach (Tanjung Papuma) is a white-sand beach with dramatic offshore
rock formations and a rocky cape headland in Jember Regency, East Java. It is
the only coastal destination on JVTO's circuits, used as a change of pace
between volcano and waterfall days on longer itineraries.

# Visiting

- A short, easy walk up the headland gives elevated views over the rocks and
  coastline; no special permits or health screening are required.
- Conditions are calmest in the dry season.
